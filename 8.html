ALL ABOUT CAULIFLOWER CURRY

Cauliflower curry is a popular Indian dish that is loved by many for its flavorful and aromatic taste. This vegetarian dish is made with cauliflower florets cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based curry sauce. It is a staple in Indian cuisine and is enjoyed as a main course or as a side dish with rice or bread.

Recipe

To make Cauliflower curry, you would require ingredients:

  • 1 medium-sized cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder and 1/2 cup malai
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once they start to splutter, add the chopped onions and saut√© until they turn translucent. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute. Then, add the pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates from the sides.
  2. Add the coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and red chili powder. Stir well and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add the cauliflower florets and mix well with the spice mixture. Cook for 5 minutes on medium heat.
  3. Pour in 1 cup of water and bring it to a boil. Then, reduce the heat and let it simmer until the cauliflower is cooked and tender.
  4. Add the heavy cream and salt to taste. Mix well and let it simmer for another 2-3 minutes. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or bread.

Place of Origin

Cauliflower curry, also known as gobi masala, has its roots in North Indian cuisine. It is believed to have originated in the Punjab region and has now become a popular dish all over the country. With the increasing popularity of Indian cuisine worldwide, cauliflower curry has also gained recognition and is now a common dish in Indian restaurants around the world.
