ALL ABOUT MIX VEG CURRY

Mix veg curry, also known as mixed vegetable curry, is a popular Indian dish that is loved by people all over the world. It is a delicious and healthy dish that is filled with the goodness of various vegetables and a flavorful mix of spices. This dish is not only popular in India, but it has also gained popularity in other countries due to its unique and delectable taste.

Recipe

To make Mix veg curry, you would require ingredients:

  • 2 cups of mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, cauliflower, potatoes, beans)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder and 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder and 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 cup water and fresh coriander leaves, for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Wash and chop all the vegetables into bite-sized pieces. You can use any vegetables of your choice, but the traditional mix veg curry usually includes carrots, potatoes, cauliflower, green beans, and peas. In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Then, add the chopped onion and sauté until it turns golden brown. Next, add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped green chili (if using). Sauté for a minute.
  2. Adding the spices Add all the dry spices – coriander powder, garam masala powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt – to the pan and mix well. Sauté for a minute to release the flavors of the spices.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes to the pan and cook until they turn soft and mushy. Then, add the chopped vegetables and mix well with the spices. Add 1 cup of water and cover the pan with a lid. Let the vegetables cook for 10-12 minutes or until they are tender.
  4. Once the vegetables are cooked, remove the lid and let the curry simmer for another 2-3 minutes to thicken the sauce. If the curry is too thick, you can add a little bit of water to adjust the consistency. Finally, garnish with fresh coriander leaves and turn off the heat. Your delicious mix veg curry is now ready to be served hot with rice, roti, or naan bread.

Place of Origin

Mix veg curry has its roots in northern India, specifically in the state of Punjab. It is a staple dish in Punjabi cuisine and is also widely popular in other parts of the country. This dish has been passed down through generations and has evolved with time, incorporating different spices and techniques to enhance its flavor.
